OUR SERVICES

What we do.

LK Cleaning services was founded in 2019. Since then we have grown an excellent reputation around the local area for our high standards and reliable service.

We use the latest water fed pole technology and ultra pure water leaving a streak free finish every time.

We also offer a number of other services including gutter clearance and fascia cleaning.

Window Cleaning
We offer a reliable, regular window cleaning service. Using the latest technology to get your windows perfectly cleaned every time.
Gutter Cleaning
We use our Gutter vacuum to clear all debris and muck from you guttering. Using the vacuum we can reach even the most difficult of guttering.
Fascia Cleaning
Using our water fed pole and a light chemical we will have you fascia and exterior guttering looking brand new again.
Conservatory Cleaning
We get you conservatory roof and windows looking brand new again. Removing all of the moss and algae growth.
